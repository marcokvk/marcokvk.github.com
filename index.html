Se Feliz Haz Feliz

Biográfica
2009
clothing / identity / packaging / web
This is a project that I undertook while working at studio Biografica. The name of the brand is “se feliz haz feliz” which roughly translates as: be happy make others happy. This is the studio motto. We wanted this attitude to stand out in the final product, by using earth friendly materials, like organic cotton and bamboo for most of the t-shirts as well as natural dyes. I worked in many different pieces for the project, and also managed the production and distribution of the t-shirts. Through this project I not only got to learn a lot in the field of design but also about the impact that the clothes we wear have on the environment.
